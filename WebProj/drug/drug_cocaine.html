    Cocaine

    Cocaine, a.k.a coke, is a strong stimulant mostly used as a recreational drug. It is commonly snorted, inhaled, or injected into the veins. Mental effects may include loss of contact with reality, an intense feeling of happiness, or agitation. Physical symptons may include a fast heart rate, sweating and large pupils. High doses can result in very high blood pressure or body temperature. Effects begin within seconds to minutes of use and last between five to ninety minutes. Although it sounds dangerous, it has a small number of accepted medical users such as numbing and decreasing bleeding during nasal surgery.
